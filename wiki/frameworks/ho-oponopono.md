# Ho'oponopono

## Overview

**Purpose**: Healing through taking responsibility for what we perceive

**Origin**: Hawaiian Kahuna tradition


## Core Principles

1. **I'm sorry, Please forgive me, Thank you, I love you**
2. **What we perceive in others is in ourselves**
3. **Healing the self heals the world**
